From Savanna to Table: Celebrating African Cuisine

Fish

Cooked fish has a tender, flaky texture with a mild, savory flavor. Its taste can range from sweet and buttery to slightly briny, depending on the type. Cooking methods like grilling, baking, or steaming enhance its natural oils, making it moist and flavorful.

Githeri

Githeri is a traditional Kenyan dish made from a mix of maize and beans, often cooked with vegetables like potatoes, carrots, and greens. It's a hearty, nutritious meal, typically seasoned with onions, tomatoes, and spices for added flavor. The dish is versatile and can be enjoyed with meat or as a vegetarian option.

Pilau

Pilau is a flavorful rice dish typically made with spices like cumin, cinnamon, and cardamom, giving it a rich, aromatic taste. It is often cooked with meat, such as chicken or beef, and sometimes includes vegetables or raisins for added texture and sweetness. The rice absorbs all the spices and flavors, creating a deliciously savory and satisfying meal.
